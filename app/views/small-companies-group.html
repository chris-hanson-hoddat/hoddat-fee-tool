Members of ineligible groups

If a group of companies includes any of the following, all companies in the group are ineligible for the small companies regime under the Companies Act 2006:

  • traded companies
  • body corporates (other than companies) whose shares are admitted to trading on a regulated market in an EEA State
  • people (other than small companies) who have permission under Part 4 of the Financial Services and Markets Act 2000 (c. 8) to carry on a regulated activity
  • e-money issuers
  • small companies that are authorised insurance companies, banking companies, Markets in Financial Instruments Directive 2004/39/EC (MiFID) investment firms or Undertakings for Collective Investment in Transferable Securities (UCITS) management companies
  • people who carry on insurance market activity
