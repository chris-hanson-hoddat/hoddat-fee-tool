Groups whose members always pay the larger fee

If your organisation is part of a group you may need to pay the larger fee of £1,476.

All the members of a group pay the larger fee if any member is:

  • a traded company
  • a body corporate (other than a company) whose shares are admitted to trading on a regulated market in an EEA State
  • a person (other than a small company) who has permission under Part 4 of the Financial Services and Markets Act 2000 (c. 8) to carry on a regulated activity
  • an authorised insurance company, a banking company, an e-money issuer, a MiFID investment firm or a UCITS management company
  • a person who carries on insurance market activity

At any time in your last full financial year, have you been part of a group that includes any of these?
